# Glossary - Key RICOH360 Terms

> Alphabetical reference of key terms used across the RICOH360 platform. Each entry provides a one-line definition in plain language.

---

**Album**
A container for grouping related 360 photos and videos. Albums can represent a site, a visit, a project phase, or any grouping that makes sense for your workflow.

**Annotation**
A marker placed directly on a 360 panorama to point out and describe a specific detail. Annotations include a title and description. They are visible to team members but not to external recipients of shared links.

**App Connection**
The marketplace section in the RICOH360 web app where you can connect third-party partner applications to your RICOH360 account.

**Auto-Upload**
The automatic transfer of captured photos and videos from the THETA camera to the RICOH360 cloud. This happens whenever the camera connects to a saved WiFi network with internet access.

**Business Pack**
The main B2B subscription offer from Ricoh Futures EMEA. Each license includes a THETA camera (rental), 10 user accounts, 100 GB of cloud storage, platform access, and Ricoh Care hardware coverage.

**Business Pack Core**
A Business Pack variant that includes a THETA A1 or THETA X camera. Designed for standard capture workflows using the mobile app or physical shutter button.

**Business Pack Type S**
A Business Pack variant that includes a THETA X camera configured with the Simple Capture plugin. Designed for guided, screen-based capture workflows.

**Crop Perspective**
An AI processing feature that extracts a flat 2D view from a specific angle within a 360 panorama, similar to cropping a traditional photo from the full sphere.

**Enhancement**
An AI processing feature that automatically improves the visual quality of a 360 panorama by adjusting brightness, contrast, and applying super resolution.

**Face Blur**
An AI processing feature that automatically detects and blurs human faces in a 360 panorama for privacy compliance.

**Field Automation**
An advanced module for automated capture workflows with path tracking and coordinate mapping. Used in integration scenarios with BIM and digital twin partners.

**Floor Plan**
An image (PNG, JPG, or SVG) uploaded to a project to represent a physical space. Markers are placed on the floor plan to show where each 360 panorama was captured.

**Gallery**
The main section of the web app that displays all 360 photos and videos captured by your team, sorted chronologically.

**HDR (High Dynamic Range)**
A capture mode that combines multiple exposures into a single image. Produces better results in mixed lighting conditions (bright windows and dark interiors in the same shot). Available in standard HDR and HDR Handheld modes.

**Marker**
A pin placed on a floor plan within a project. Each marker links to one or more 360 panoramas captured at that location.

**Panorama**
A 360-degree photo that captures the full sphere around the camera. You can navigate inside a panorama by clicking and dragging to look in any direction.

**Premium Pack**
A hardware-as-a-service subscription that provides a THETA camera rental with Ricoh Care coverage and accessories. Does not include platform licenses (those are purchased separately or through a Business Pack).

**Project**
A workspace in the web app that uses floor plans to organize captures spatially. Projects show where each panorama was taken on a map of your site.

**Ricoh Care**
The hardware coverage program included with Business Pack and Premium Pack subscriptions. Covers one camera replacement per contract period for manufacturing defects or damage during normal professional use. Does not cover loss, theft, or accessories.

**Side-by-Side**
A viewer mode that displays two 360 panoramas next to each other for visual comparison. Useful for before-and-after documentation or progress tracking.

**Simple Capture**
A plugin installed on THETA X cameras that replaces the standard interface with a simplified screen showing album selection and a capture button. Designed for operators who do not need technical camera controls.

**Super Resolution**
An AI processing step within Enhancement that increases the perceived detail and sharpness of a 360 panorama.

**THETA Management**
The admin console section where team administrators can view camera status, battery levels, firmware versions, and manage album assignments for Simple Capture.

**THETA Twin**
The digital representation of a physical THETA camera in the cloud. The Twin shows the camera's current status (battery, storage, firmware, connectivity) and allows remote configuration.

**Type S**
Short for "Business Pack Type S." Refers to the Simple Capture configuration of the Business Pack, where the THETA X camera runs the guided capture plugin.

**Viewer**
The interactive 360-degree viewer in the RICOH360 web app and mobile app. Allows you to navigate inside panoramas, zoom, annotate, draw, and compare captures.

**Zone**
A section within a project, typically representing a floor or area of a building. Each zone can have its own floor plan and set of markers.
