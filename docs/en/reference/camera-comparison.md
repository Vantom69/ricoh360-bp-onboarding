# Camera Comparison - THETA X vs THETA A1 vs THETA Z1

> This guide helps you understand the differences between the three RICOH THETA 360 camera models and choose the right one for your workflow. The THETA X and THETA A1 are available through Business Pack and Premium Pack subscriptions. The THETA Z1 is available for direct purchase only.

## When to Choose THETA X

The THETA X is the default recommendation for most professional deployments.

**Choose THETA X when:**
- You need a touchscreen interface for on-device control and preview
- You plan to use Simple Capture for guided, operator-friendly workflows
- You want a swappable battery for long field days (carry a spare)
- You need removable SD card storage for flexible capacity
- You are deploying multiple cameras across teams (scalable, easy to manage)
- Your captures are primarily 360 photos with occasional short video

**Best for:** Site documentation, facility management, real estate, standard inspection workflows, multi-camera team deployments.

---

## When to Choose THETA A1

The THETA A1 is built for demanding operational environments.

**Choose THETA A1 when:**
- You need long video recording sessions (up to 120 minutes continuously)
- You work in dusty, wet, or extreme temperature conditions (IP64, -10C to 40C)
- You need a durable camera that can handle rough handling on construction sites
- Screen-free operation is acceptable (operators use the physical shutter button or mobile app)

**Important:** The THETA A1 is available through rental only (Business Pack or Premium Pack). It cannot be purchased outright.

**Best for:** Construction site documentation, infrastructure inspection, outdoor monitoring, long-duration video capture.

---

## When to Choose THETA Z1

The THETA Z1 is a specialist imaging device for maximum visual quality.

**Choose THETA Z1 when:**
- Image quality is your top priority (dual 1-inch sensors capture significantly more detail and light)
- You frequently shoot in low-light environments (basements, warehouses, evening conditions)
- You need RAW file support (DNG) for post-processing
- You work in professional photography or high-end documentation where image fidelity matters most

**Important:** The THETA Z1 is not included in Business Pack or Premium Pack subscriptions. It is available for direct purchase only. It does not support Simple Capture.

**Best for:** Professional photography, low-light environments, heritage documentation, high-quality visual archives.

## Quick Decision Guide

| Your priority | Recommended model |
|--------------|-------------------|
| General-purpose professional use | THETA X |
| Touchscreen and Simple Capture | THETA X |
| Long video recording (>25 min) | THETA A1 |
| Harsh environments (dust, rain, cold) | THETA A1 |
| Best image quality and low-light | THETA Z1 |
| RAW file output for editing | THETA Z1 |
| Multi-camera fleet deployment | THETA X |
| Budget-conscious team rollout | THETA X |
