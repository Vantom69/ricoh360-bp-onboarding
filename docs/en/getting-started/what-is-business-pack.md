# What Is the Business Pack?

> Everything you need to start capturing, organizing, and sharing 360 content with your team, in one subscription.

The RICOH360 Business Pack is a bundled offer that combines hardware, software, cloud storage, and support into a single monthly subscription. Instead of buying a camera, subscribing to software, and setting up support separately, the Business Pack gives you everything as one operational package.

---

## What's Included in One License

A single Business Pack license includes three components:

### 1. RICOH THETA Camera (Rental)

A professional 360 camera included in your subscription. You do not purchase the camera; it is part of the service.

| Pack Type | Camera | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Business Pack Core** | THETA X or THETA A1 | Standard deployment, choose based on your needs |
| **Business Pack Type S** | THETA X (required) | Includes Simple Capture: guided workflow on the camera screen |

The camera remains Ricoh property for the duration of the contract. If you cancel, you return it.

**Not sure which camera?** See the [Camera Comparison](/en/reference/camera-comparison) guide and the camera recommendation sections in the [Use Case Guides](/en/use-cases/site-documentation).

### 2. RICOH360 Premium (Software + Cloud)

Full access to the RICOH360 platform with premium features:

| What you get | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **10 user accounts** | Invite your team. Each member gets full access to the web app and mobile app. |
| **100 GB cloud storage** | Enough for several thousand 360 panoramas. Shared across all team members. |
| **Projects and Floor Plans** | Organize photos spatially on building plans. [PREMIUM] |
| **Annotations** | Add notes, markers, and drawings directly on panoramas. [PREMIUM] |
| **Simple Capture** | Guided capture workflow on the THETA X screen (Type S packs). [PREMIUM] |
| **App Connection** | Access to partner integrations via the marketplace. [PREMIUM] |
| **Team Management** | Admin console to manage members, roles, cameras, and storage. [PREMIUM] |

You also get all features available on the free plan:

| Free features included | Details |
|----------------------|---------|
| Gallery and Albums | Upload, organize, and browse all your 360 content |
| 360 Viewer | Navigate, zoom, and explore panoramas in the browser |

### 3. Ricoh Care (Support + Protection)

Hardware protection included at no extra cost:

| What Ricoh Care covers | Details |
|------------------------|---------|
| **1 free camera replacement per year** | If your camera is damaged or has a hardware failure, Ricoh sends a replacement. |
| **Transport covered both ways** | Ricoh pays for shipping the replacement to you and for returning the damaged unit. |
| **Process** | Contact your Ricoh account manager. A replacement ships quickly. Return the damaged camera in the same box. |

**What Ricoh Care does NOT cover:**
- Loss or theft of the camera
- Accessories (monopod, case, lens cap, cables)
- Damage caused by use outside operating conditions

For repairs outside Ricoh Care coverage, see the [Repair page](https://support.ricoh360.com/fr/repair) on the Help Center.

## How It Works

The Business Pack is a **12-month subscription** billed monthly. There is no large upfront investment.

- **Monthly cost:** Starting from 80 EUR/month per license (varies by configuration)
- **Commitment:** 12 months minimum
- **Hardware:** Included in the subscription (rental, not purchase)
- **Scaling:** Need more capacity? Add licenses. Each license adds 10 users, 100 GB, and 1 camera.

### One License = One Collaborative Unit

A license is not designed for a single user. It is built for a team:
- 10 people can access and collaborate on the same 360 content
- Field operators capture, office teams organize and share
- One admin manages the team, cameras, and storage

## Business Pack Core vs Type S

| | Core | Type S |
|---|------|--------|
| Camera options | THETA X or THETA A1 | THETA X only |
| Simple Capture | No | Yes |
| Target users | Teams comfortable with the mobile app | Teams with non-technical field operators |
| Capture workflow | Use the mobile app to trigger and transfer | Select album on camera screen, press button, auto-upload |

**When to choose Type S:** Your field operators are not comfortable with smartphones, or you want a standardized, foolproof capture process (select album, press shutter, done). See [Simple Capture Setup](/en/getting-started/simple-capture-setup).

**When to choose Core:** Your team is comfortable using the mobile app, or you need the THETA A1 for harsh environments or long video recording.
