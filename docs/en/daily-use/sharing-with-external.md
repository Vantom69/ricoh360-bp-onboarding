# Sharing with External Stakeholders

> Learn how to share 360 content with people outside your team, what they can and cannot see, and when to use team invitation instead.

---

## How Sharing Works

RICOH360 lets you share individual 360 panoramas or videos with anyone via a public link. The recipient does not need a RICOH360 account. They simply click the link and view the 360 content in their browser.

This is useful for sending a single capture to a client, a subcontractor, an insurer, or any external party who needs to see a specific location.

---

## Generating a Share Link: Step by Step

1. Open the panorama or video you want to share.
2. Click the **Share** button in the Viewer toolbar.
3. The media must be set to **public** status to generate a link. Toggle this if it is not already enabled.
4. Copy the generated link.
5. Send the link via email, messaging app, or any communication channel.

![Share link dialog with Public/Private toggle](/images/webapp/sharing_modal.png)

The recipient clicks the link and sees the 360 content in an embedded viewer. They can navigate the panorama (click-drag, zoom) just like you would, but without access to any other platform features.

---

## What External Viewers See

When someone opens a share link, they get:

- Full 360 navigation (click-drag, zoom, auto-rotation)
- The panorama or video in its current state

That is it.

---

## What External Viewers Do NOT See

Share links are intentionally limited. External viewers cannot see or access:

- **Annotations.** Any hotspot annotations or drawings you placed on the panorama are stripped from the shared view. The recipient sees the raw 360 image only.
- **Other media.** The link gives access to one specific panorama or video. They cannot browse your gallery, albums, or projects.
- **Floor plans or project context.** Projects with floor plans and markers are not accessible through share links.
- **Team information.** No visibility into your team members, other captures, or organizational structure.

---

## Current Sharing Limitations

Be aware of these constraints when using share links:

| Limitation | What it means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Individual media only** | You cannot share an entire album or a selection of multiple photos with a single link. Each share link covers one panorama or one video. |
| **No password protection** | Links are public. Anyone with the link can view the content. There is no option to add a password. |
| **No expiration date** | Once created, a share link remains active until you manually revoke it by setting the media back to private. There is no automatic expiry. |
| **Annotations not included** | Hotspot annotations and drawings do not appear in the shared view. If you need to show marked-up content to an external party, consider taking a screenshot of the annotated view instead. |
| **No project sharing** | You cannot share a project (floor plan with positioned markers) via a link. External parties must be invited as team members to access project views. |

---

## Team Invitation: The Alternative for Ongoing Collaboration

If a share link is too limited for your needs, consider inviting the external person as a **team member**.

Team members get full access to the platform (based on their assigned role: admin or member). This includes:

- Browsing all photos, videos, and albums
- Viewing annotations
- Accessing projects and floor plans
- Using the full Viewer with all tools

This approach is better suited for:

- Subcontractors who need to review multiple captures regularly
- Clients involved in ongoing project reviews
- Partners who need to annotate or document alongside your team

Each Business Pack license includes **10 user accounts**. Additional collaborators count against this limit.

---

## Embedding the Viewer (Technical Users)

For teams with technical resources, RICOH360 offers a **Viewer SDK** that lets you embed 360 panoramas directly into your own web applications, portals, or internal tools.

This is an advanced option. It requires development work but gives you full control over how the 360 content is presented and integrated into your workflows. Speak with your Ricoh Futures EMEA contact if this interests you.
